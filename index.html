Welcome to EXProc

EXProc is intended to become a community initiative to provide extensions for XProc, as EXSLT provides extensions for XSLT.

Extensions are divided into two classes, proposed extensions and accepted extensions.

Your suggestions most welcome.

The best place to discuss XProc extensions is the xproc-dev mailing list.
